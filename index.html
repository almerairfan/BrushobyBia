Hi, I'm Bia — a self-taught canvas painter obsessed with colour, texture, and the quiet magic that happens when a brush meets canvas.

I take custom commissions from people who want something truly personal — a portrait, a vibe, a memory, or just a feeling they've been carrying around and want to see brought to life.

Share your reference image or describe your idea, and I'll handle the rest. Every piece is painted by hand, with love, just for you.
